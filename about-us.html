About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2024, the Bergen Community Science Museum was created with a simple vision: to provide a space where science comes to life. What began as a local initiative has grown into a thriving hub for families, students, educators, and science enthusiasts from all over Norway and beyond. Each year, we welcome thousands of visitors, each with a shared passion for discovery and innovation.

Our Team

Our team is made up of dedicated professionals who are committed to making science accessible to all. From our educators and researchers to our administrative staff, each member of our team plays a vital role in bringing our mission to life. Together, we work to create engaging exhibits, develop educational programs, and foster a sense of wonder and curiosity in all who visit our museum.
